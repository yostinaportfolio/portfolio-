Academic Project · Giza, Egypt

Ankh School of Restoration & Archaeology

عنخ — «إحياء الماضي… من أجل المستقبل»

Inspired by the layout of the pyramids, this design reinterprets the iconic form while incorporating elements of the ancient Egyptian Key of Life — creating a concept rooted in heritage and expressive in architectural identity.

Located in Giza, ANKH is a secondary school dedicated to restoration and archaeology. The project aims to preserve cultural heritage by educating future generations in conservation, research, and archaeological sciences.

Hospitality · Alexandria, Egypt

Aetheria Hotel

"Aetheria" is a three-star hotel located along the Sidi Gaber Corniche in Alexandria, designed as a destination that combines hospitality, culture, and community.

Inspired by the movement of sea waves and the elegance of Ionic Greek columns, the project reflects Alexandria's Mediterranean identity while creating a modern and welcoming atmosphere.

Aetheria — Waterfront Perspective

Design Concept

Inspired by the rhythm of sea waves and the timeless elegance of Alexandria's Greek heritage, Aetheria creates a balance between movement, light, and hospitality — connecting people, culture, and the Mediterranean atmosphere.
